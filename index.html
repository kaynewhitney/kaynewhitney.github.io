KAYNE WHITNEY

Cybersecurity • Threat Hunting • Incident Response

Kayne Whitney is a cybersecurity professional and student specializing in security operations, threat hunting, and incident response. He currently works as a cyber security analyst for the State of Wisconsin and serves as a cybersecurity consultant for a consulting company.

Academically, Kayne studies Cybersecurity at the University of Wisconsin–Stout. He is a transfer student from Madison College, where he earned his associate degree with academic honors as a cybersecurity specialist. At UW–Stout, he serves multiple roles. He is a Director on the Student Senate, is the Cybersecurity Program Ambassador, and is the Vice President of the Cybersecurity Club. In the cybersecurity club he leads as team captain for the University's National Cyber League team and also competes on the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition team as the Linux Threat Hunter.

In his free time, he enjoys archery, long-distance running, 3D printing, and weightlifting.
